Internet 2.0

Participative and Social Web

In early 2000’s the world wide web was accessible in a very large scale which led to how world started changing the web. Because of the speed progress in e-commerce and connectivity People have really used it to their advantage to grow themselves on the internet. With the web 2.0 revolution lead by Hurley, Rose, and Adelson – YouTube, Facebook, blogs took dominance as the users decided what each other users voted for and wanted to see. Not just with YouTube or Facebook but with the advancement in social media platforms where broadcasting one’s life,interacting and sharing information is becoming the norm and it’s all available at your fingertips.

Concerns with the New Era Internet

This new era of Internet is both revolutionizing and concerning as there are several issues that require awareness and attention. Some of these issues include equal access, information control, intellectual property, copyright, authorship, trust, privacy, and security. Hopefully with adequate regulatory policies and governance adapting these technologies in a more humanly integrated way is very plausible.
